This is the first commit for Employee training system.

Brief about what I have done:
Trainer registers and logs in.
He can add, edit and cancel(if there are not any bookings against his/her module) his/her training module.
He can view his trainings
He can view the reservations on his trainings
I have also added search by topic feature to search trainer's  training modules.

Employee registers and logs in.
He can search by topic all training modules posted by all trainers.
He can book a seat and cancel a seat against a training module.
He can view his trainings
